Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2024

Overview

Loan Manager Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Loan Manager Pro").

By using Loan Manager Pro, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information

  • Debtor and creditor details including names, phone numbers, and loan information you enter into the app.
  • Optional document uploads (PDFs, images) associated with loans.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data such as features used, actions taken, and timestamps (for analytics and improvements).
  • Device information (model, OS version) for crash reporting and performance optimization.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality: managing loans, tracking payments, and generating invoices.
  • To improve the application through usage analytics and crash reports.
  • To send optional push notifications (if enabled).

Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except in these limited cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., court orders)
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating the app (under strict confidentiality)

Data Storage & Security

Your loan data is stored securely on your device and (if enabled) backed up via your personal loan manager account. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available within the app and on our website. Continued use of Loan Manager Pro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
